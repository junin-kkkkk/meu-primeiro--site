Historia dos Macacos

Monkey is a term of African origin used as a common designation for all species of apes or anthropoid primates. It is applied restrictively in Brazil to cebids in general. In the strict sense, "monkey" refers to the primate species belonging to the genus Macaca.

Tudo sobre macaco

Monkeys are mammals of the primate group. The order Primates is divided into two suborders: Strepsirrhini and Haplorrhini. The first includes lemurs, lorises and galagoes, while the second includes tarsiers and anthropoids.
